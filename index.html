ABOUT

Great Mistakes is a band that thrives on Great Mistakes writes songs that land between melody and grit, blending the drive of punk with the heart of rock. The result is honest, unpolished, and built to connect. Made up of longtime friends and veterans of bands including The Saint Alvia Cartel, Grade, The Reason, The Artist Life, The Spicoli’s, The Video Dead, Jersey, Catapult, and Always Outnumbered, the group brings years of shared stages and stories into something new. For fans with tastes ranging from Hot Water Music and The Dirty Nil to The Gaslight Anthem and Rival Schools, Great Mistakes leans into big choruses, raw guitars, and lyrics that don’t shy away from the heavier corners of life.
